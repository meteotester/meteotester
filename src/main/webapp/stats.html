How accurate are weather forecasts?

In the following charts forecast accuracy is presented in terms of Root Mean Square Error. So lower values are better. And 0 would be perfect score. A baseline benchmark is included corresponding to trivial predictions: forecasts for the next 5 days being the same as today's weather.

Precipitation forecasts error applies to predicted probabilities (range: 0 to 1). It is equivalent to the square root of Brier Score.
