Testing and comparing weather forecasts

MeteoTester starts on August 2013. It is intended to collect and test forecasts published by any meteorological data source. Â¿Why?

  • To compare weather information from different providers.
  • To find the most reliable weather forecasts.
  • To report current limitations of predictions in terms of accuracy.
  • To establish a public transparent benchmark for weather forecast verification.
  • To improve weather forecasts.

For the moment, MeteoTester indexes the following meteorological providers:

  • forecast.io
  • openweathermap.org
  • wunderground.com
  • worldweatheronline.com

More coming soon...
